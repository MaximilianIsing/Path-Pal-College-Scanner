Our Team

Brody Rosenblatt

Co-Founder

I co-founded PathPal because I wanted to solve the same problems I faced as a high school student. I remember anxiously waiting to see if I would get into my dream school with almost no insight or real guidance. That experience showed me how stressful and confusing the college process can be when you're navigating it blindly. With PathPal, we set out to give students the clarity, structure, and confidence that I wish I had. Our goal is to make academic and career planning more transparent, more personalized, and ultimately more empowering for every student.
